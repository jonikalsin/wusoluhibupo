How Many Children Does Patti LaBelle Have and Who are They?

Patti LaBelle is a mother of five children named Zuri Kye Edwards, Dodd-Stocker Edwards, Stanley Stocker-Edwards, William Holte, and Stayce Holte. 

Interestingly, four of Patti LaBelle’s children were adopted; the revered superstar has only one biological child. Fondly referred to as the “Godmother of Soul”, Patti has loved her adopted children in the very same way she loved her biological child. Over the years, she has raised them to become very responsible men and women in society.

Zuri Kye Edwards Is Patti LaBelle’s Only Biological Child

  • Date of birth: July 17, 1973
  • Age: 50 years old
  • Father: Armstead Edwards
  • Occupation: Talent manager

Zuri Kye Edwards is the only biological son of Patti LaBelle and her ex-husband, Armstead Edwards. Zuri was born on July 17, 1973, and is now 50 years old. He was born about four years after his parents got married. According to reports, Zuri was born in the United States. However, it is not clear what part of the USA, he was born in.

What is clear, however, is that he had a great upbringing and had the best of what every child could hope to have. At the time he was born, his famous mother had already become a successful musician and so he was apparently born into privilege. He would often follow his mother on the road when she toured and saw firsthand how things were run in the music business while still a child.

Details about the schools that Zuri Kye Edwards attended are not very clear but it is believed that he had access to the best school money could afford considering the son of whom he is. Growing up, he became very fond of his mother and was always seen beside her. Also, the fact that he grew up watching his mother perform at shows and star in movies, made him develop some interest in the entertainment industry.

But, even though he developed an interest in music early in his life, Zuri Kye did not follow in his mother’s footsteps in pursuing a career as a singer in the music industry. Instead, he took a different path; he actually forged his own career path by becoming a talent manager, helping artists build their careers and reach their full potential.

He Is Now His Mother’s Manager

Not only is Zuri Kye Edwards a talent manager, but he also has, as a matter of fact, even taken on the role of being his mother’s manager, navigating her career and helping her stay relevant in the ever-changing music industry. Zuri Kye became his mother’s manager after she parted ways with his father, Edwards, and he has been going great in the role.

According to our findings, Zuri Kye’s relationship with his mother has not always been smooth sailing, but they have managed to maintain a healthy and productive work relationship. Patti LaBelle herself once acknowledged, during an interview with Closer Weekly, that working with her son can be challenging because sometimes he hurts her by always being brutally honest and this can lead to fights. However, she also explained that she has come to realize that at the end of the day, he gives her the best advice ever and is never going to lead her wrong.

Without a doubt, one of the biggest fears of celebrity kids is that they would never escape the shadows of their famous parents when they venture out to make a name for themselves. Zuri is well aware of this. But, despite the challenges of growing up in the shadow of a famous mother, he has been able to forge his own path and has built a successful career, helping artists reach their full potential and bringing his mother’s music to new audiences.

It is also noteworthy that despite his success as a talent manager, Zuri Kye Edwards has largely stayed out of the public eye, choosing to focus on his work in managing people. However, he has made occasional appearances on the big screen, supporting his mother in her endeavors. In the 2015 TV series Patti LaBelle’s Place, Zuri Kye appeared as a guest, joining his mother in entertaining and cooking for their guests. This was a testament to their close relationship and the love they have for each other.

Zuri Kye also appeared in the 2000s docu-series titled Journeys in Black, which chronicled the lives of famous African Americans, including Kirk Franklin, father of Kerrion Franklin. Zuri Kye was able to share his insights on the experiences of growing up in a famous family and the challenges of navigating the entertainment industry. He portrayed his mother in an awesome light, talking about what a wonderful person she has always been.

In 1987, Zuri Kye was also featured in the documentary series Biography, which detailed the lives of historical figures and celebrities. This was another platform where he was able to share his unique perspective on growing up with a famous mother and the impact it had on his life.

But, despite his occasional appearances on screen, Zuri Kye’s primary focus remains on his work as a talent manager, helping artists reach their full potential and make their mark in the music industry. His dedication to his work and his mother’s career is a testament to the love and support he has for his family, and his contributions have helped ensure that Patti LaBelle remains a beloved icon in the music industry.

Zuri Kye Edwards Is Married To Lona Azami

Zuri Kye Edwards is currently a married man. He is married to a woman called Lona Azami. Although information about where they met and started their relationship are not clear, details we gleaned from Lona’s Instagram page reveal that they got married on the 11th of October 2014.

The couple has remained together ever since and it appears that their love seems to be growing every day. A quick look at Lona’s Instagram page shows that he and his wife are deeply into each other. They regularly take vacations and spend lots of time with each other. Lona has described Zuri as her ‘entire world’.

Together, the couple now has three children; two daughters named Gia and Leyla, and a son called Zuri Jr. Their daughter Gia was born on March 3, 2015, while Leyla was born on September 15, 2017, and ZURI Jr. was born in December 2021.

Patti LaBelle, who is clearly a proud grandmother, announced the birth of Zuri on social media, sharing an adorable picture of the newborn. She described Zuri Jr. as “one of the best gifts ever” of her holiday season, highlighting the strong bond she has with her grandchildren. In a 2018 interview, Patti opened up about how the presence of her grandchildren has changed her holiday celebrations.

With Gia already three years old at the time, Patti revealed that her granddaughter loved helping her cook in the kitchen. She also shared that she enjoyed letting her grandchildren “make a fool out of her,” saying that they were free to play with her jewelry and other belongings, even if it meant breaking things. This shows the close relationship between Patti and her grandchildren, and the joy they bring to her life.

While Zuri Kye Edwards’ personal life remains mostly private, it is clear that he values family and cherishes his role as a husband and father. His dedication to his family is reflected in the close bond they share with his mother, Patti LaBelle, who is a prominent figure in their lives. Together, they continue to make memories and build a legacy of love and support for generations to come
